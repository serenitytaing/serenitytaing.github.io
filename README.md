<h2>About Our Club:</h2>

<p>Club Chang'an is the Chinese cultural club here on campus, dedicated to spreading awareness and appreciation of Chinese culture. Our mission is to promote cultural exchange and foster an understanding of Chinese heritage through a wide variety of activities, performances, and social events. Whether you're already familiar with Chinese traditions or are simply curious about learning more, our club provides a welcoming environment where you can engage with Chinese culture in a fun and interactive way.</p>

<p>We organize events throughout the year to showcase the beauty of Chinese customs and traditions, such as dance performances, music showcases, cooking workshops, and celebrations of major festivals like Lunar New Year. Club Chang'an is a diverse group of students passionate about sharing their love for Chinese culture while building a close-knit community. We value inclusivity and encourage anyone with an interest in cultural exchange to join us, regardless of background or experience. You'll not only learn about ancient and modern Chinese culture, but you'll also gain leadership experience, make new friends, and create lasting memories! </p>
<p>Through participation in Club Chang'an, you'll have the chance to experience Chinese arts and crafts, cuisine, and language. We often host workshops where members can learn traditional calligraphy, paper cutting, and even Chinese martial arts. Our goal is to provide a comprehensive and immersive experience in Chinese culture while making sure everyone has fun along the way. Whether you're looking to deepen your understanding of Chinese heritage or simply want to participate in engaging activities, we are here to help you explore, learn, and grow. So, what are you waiting for? Join us and become part of a cultural adventure!</p>

<h2>Talent Show!</h2>
    <ul>
        <li>Our club has won the 2022-2023 talent show!</li>
        <li>Our club is well-known for its exciting performances at the annual school talent show. From traditional Chinese dances, such as the elegant fan dance, to modern performances that blend contemporary pop culture with Chinese elements, we strive to make each performance an unforgettable experience. </li>
        <li>Each dance follows a traditional Chinese story crafted by our competition heads, and we tell this rich narrative through our choreography. Members of all skill levels are encouraged to take part, allowing everyone to contribute to this unique storytelling through dance.</li>
    </ul>
  

<h2>International Day</h2>
    <p>
      International Day is a highlight of our year, allowing us to showcase Chinese culture to the school community. We perform both traditional and modern dances.
 </p>
